Our Services

Professional signs, banners, and commercial vehicle wraps — designed, printed, and installed by a family shop with 30+ years of experience.

Service 01

Custom Signs

Your sign is often the first impression a customer gets of your business. We build custom signage that makes that impression count — crafted with premium materials and designed to fit your brand, your building, and your budget.


Popular sign projects include: storefront and building signs, dimensional and channel letters, lighted signs, yard and site signs, real estate signs, directional and wayfinding signage, window graphics and lettering, A-frames and sidewalk signs, trade show displays, and ADA-compliant interior signage.


Every sign starts with a free consultation and quote. We'll help you choose the right materials for indoor or outdoor use, handle permitting questions, and install it professionally.

Service 02

Banners

Need to announce a grand opening, promote a sale, or dress up an event? Full-color vinyl banners are the fastest, most affordable way to get a big message in front of a lot of people.


We produce: outdoor vinyl banners, indoor display banners, retractable banner stands, mesh banners for windy locations, pole banners, event and sports team banners, and banners with grommets, hems, or pole pockets for any mounting need.


Our banners are printed at high resolution on durable material that stands up to Alabama weather — vivid color that stays vivid.


Get a Banner Quote
Service 03

Commercial Vehicle Wraps

A wrapped vehicle is a billboard that works everywhere you drive. Studies consistently rank vehicle wraps among the most cost-effective advertising available — one wrap can generate tens of thousands of impressions per day.


Wrap options include: full wraps, partial wraps, cut vinyl lettering and decals, fleet graphics with consistent branding across every vehicle, trailer wraps, and magnetic signs for temporary use.


We design around your vehicle's body lines, print on premium cast vinyl, and install in-house for a smooth, professional finish that protects your paint and lasts for years.

Our Story

Family Owned. Locally Rooted.

Northport Graphics is owned and operated by Brett Booth, whose family has been in the printing and signage business for over 30 years. What started as a family trade has grown into one of West Alabama's most trusted local shops for signs, banners, and commercial vehicle graphics.


We're not a print-by-mail warehouse. When you work with Northport Graphics, you're working directly with the people who design, produce, and install your project. That means real conversations, honest advice, and work we're proud to put our name on.


From a single yard sign to a fully wrapped fleet, we treat every job with the same care — because around here, our reputation is everything.
